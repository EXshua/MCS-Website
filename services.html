Service Tiers

Compliance management that fits your operation

Three tiers of service — from essential tracking to fully managed, proactive compliance. Choose the level of cover that's right for your vessel and your operation.

Tier 1
Monitor

We watch. You act. We build and maintain your vessel's compliance register, track every expiry date and certificate, and alert you well in advance — so you're never caught out. You stay in control; we make sure nothing slips through.

Advisory Notice

MCS provides compliance management and coordination services. We track, alert, coordinate, and support — but we do not hold statutory authority and do not replace the legal obligations of the Vessel Owner or Master.

Ultimate statutory responsibility remains with the Vessel Owner / Master at all times. MCS acts as a coordination and management partner. All regulatory compliance obligations under applicable maritime legislation remain the responsibility of the operator.
