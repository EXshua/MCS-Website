The Problem

Managing compliance manually is a liability

For small vessel operators, keeping on top of safety equipment, certificates, and inspection deadlines is a constant, unglamorous job. One missed expiry can mean a failed inspection, a grounded vessel, or worse.

  • ⏰
    Expiry dates slip through

    Life rafts, EPIRB batteries, fire extinguishers — there are dozens of items with rolling expiry dates. Tracking them on spreadsheets or from memory doesn't cut it.

  • 📞
    Last-minute scrambles

    Realising something's expired the day before an inspection means panic calls to suppliers, rushed bookings, and eye-watering call-out fees.

  • 📁
    Paperwork and documentation gaps

    Certificates get lost, service records aren't up to date, and when an inspector arrives you're scrambling to find what should be immediately to hand.

  • 💸
    Avoidable costs and delays

    Equipment failures during inspections, vessels taken out of service, and emergency replacement costs are all preventable — with the right system in place.

The Solution

Zero-headache compliance, handled for you

MCS takes the ongoing burden of compliance tracking off your plate. We keep a live register of every item on your vessel, alert you well ahead of expiry, and coordinate what needs to happen next.

1
We build your vessel register

We document every piece of safety equipment, certificate, and compliance item on board with its current status and expiry date.

2
We track everything continuously

Your register stays live. We issue alerts at 90, 60, and 30 days ahead of every expiry — nothing slips through.

3
We coordinate the action

On Managed and Command tiers, we handle supplier liaison and corrective actions — you just confirm and it gets done.

4
You get a monthly summary

A clear, plain-English compliance report each month so you always know exactly where you stand.
